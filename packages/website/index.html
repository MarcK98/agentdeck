HOW IT WORKS

Three pieces. You control all of them.

01

Run the daemon

A small background process owns your Claude Code sessions, tickets, and the usage ledger — SQLite, on your Mac or a VPS. Node 20+, one command.

02

Open the app

The desktop app connects to your daemon — board, threads, map, usage. It even starts the daemon for you if it isn't running.

03

Delegate

Write a ticket, pick a model, hit go. AgentDeck drives Claude Code with your account — Anthropic bills you directly, zero markup.
